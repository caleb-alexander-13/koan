How it works

Set up in minutes.
Works in every call.

Koan sits in your Zoom sidebar and listens. When a prospect asks a question, it finds the right answer from your knowledge base and surfaces it to you — silently, instantly.

01
Connect your knowledge
Upload FAQs, pricing docs, playbooks — anything your team references. Koan indexes everything semantically.
02
Open Koan in Zoom
Install once from the Zoom App Marketplace. Open the sidebar at the start of any call.
03
Answers appear automatically
Koan detects questions in the live transcript and surfaces the right answer in seconds.
04
Close with confidence
No more "let me check on that." Your team always has the answer — instantly, silently.
